The Greek Centre (Greek Centre for Contemporary Culture) – is the cultural arm of the Greek Community of Melbourne. It operates as both a venue and as a presenter/producer.

The Centre can be found in the heart of Melbourne’s historic Greek Precinct 168 Lonsdale Street Melbourne. The Centre was completed in 2015 and its activities attract over 1400 visitors per week,
