What our students have to say

Pick the right level for you

There's no point in sitting in class to learn things you already know, so we strive to make sure you are placed in the right class-level from the beginning.

Not sure where you belong?

Just use our handy guide to figure it out for yourself.

Download this useful self-assement to start with: "Common Reference Levels - self-assessment grid"

Or speak to one of our very helpful staff to help you determine which level you belong to.

And don't worry: Made a mistake? Turns out you need to level up or down? No problem.

Pick the right time and venue

We have multiple beginner classes starting every quarter, so we are certain you find a class that fits your schedule.

Too far from Melbourne?

No worries, we run both online and hybrid classes.
